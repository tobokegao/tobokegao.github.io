About - Wed, Feb 23, 2022

What's Tobokegao?

In the summer of 2014, they started composing music using "Gameboy" as a method of musical expression that he arrived at through their band activities.

They has released many songs using "LSDj", a composition software for chiptune artists, and gained recognition.

In 2016, Joined the netlabel "ESC TRAX" and "Cheapbeats".

In 2017, Performed at the world's largest chiptune festival "Square Sounds Tokyo 2017" as their past achievements were evaluated.

In 2019, Started to operate "TBKgao", an independent label focusing on chiptune music.

In 2020, Provided bgm for the new MSX2 game "Caravan Boomer" by a gorgeous composition team.

In 2021, Provided the background music for Lily lemonade Mone, a Vsinger who jumped out of the game world by a bug belonging to 447 Records.

Currently, working on their own music production using M8 Tracker as their main tool.
